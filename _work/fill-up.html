An analysis of the inflamation of gas prices throughout the last century, juxtaposed with relevant world historical events.

Process

For this project I scoured some databases for consistent price entries and used those for my data. I then brainstormed the ways of representing that data. The iconography of the oil tower was very appealing and so I based the infographic around that, creating a place with which to chart my numbers.
